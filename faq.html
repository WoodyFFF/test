Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

+

We are open Monday to Friday from 11am to 10pm, and on weekends from 12pm to 11pm.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

+

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian burgers and sides available on our menu.

Is there parking available?

+

We have a dedicated parking lot for our customers, free of charge for up to 2 hours.

Do you take reservations?

+

Yes, we accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. Please call us or use our online booking system.

Are you wheelchair accessible?

+

Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible, including our restrooms.

Do you offer delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer delivery through popular food delivery apps and our own delivery service within a 5-mile radius.

Are your ingredients locally sourced?

+

We strive to use locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, supporting local farmers and producers.

Do you cater for private events?

+

Yes, we offer catering services for private events. Please contact us for more information and to discuss your requirements.

Is there a kids' menu available?

+

Yes, we have a dedicated kids' menu with smaller portions and child-friendly options.

Do you accommodate food allergies?

+

We take food allergies seriously and can accommodate most dietary restrictions. Please inform our staff about any allergies when ordering.
